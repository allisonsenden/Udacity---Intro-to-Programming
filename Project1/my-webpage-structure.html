Background

I grew up in Maple Grove, MN and moved to Medina, MN when I was in 6th grade. I'm now 23 years old and graduate from the University of Missouri- Columbia with a degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering. I never imagined I would grow up and want to learn to write code. As I continued to study in school and gain experience through internships, I realized that it is actually a lot more interesting and useful than I originally thought. I started working at Optum in the IT world as a newly grad and found myself really wanting to learn how to code. So here I am now, creating my own webpage with HTML code. I'm very excited to learn more, and hopefully I can apply this knowledge to many more projects in the future.
